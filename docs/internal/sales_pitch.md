 How to Improve Value Prop                                                                                              
                                                                                                                        
 ### 1. Guaranteed Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)                                                                      
                                                                                                                        
 Tell merchants: "Your cost to acquire a customer is $2-3 — guaranteed. With your own ads, you might pay $50+ per       
 customer with no guarantee."                                                                                           
                                                                                                                        
 ### 2. Local Area Expertise                                                                                            
                                                                                                                        
 - "We know which neighborhoods in Seattle respond to coffee deals"                                                     
 - "We know TikTok performs better for Gen Z, Facebook for 35+"                                                         
 - You have performance data across merchants — they don't                                                              
                                                                                                                        
 ### 3. Creative That Converts                                                                                          
                                                                                                                        
 - You A/B test QR code creative, ad copy, visuals                                                                      
 - Merchants get proven ad templates without paying an agency                                                           
                                                                                                                        
 ### 4. Customer Insights (Post-Redemption)                                                                             
                                                                                                                        
 Give merchants a dashboard showing:                                                                                    
 - Demographics of who redeemed                                                                                         
 - Peak redemption times                                                                                                
 - Repeat customer rate                                                                                                 
 - "This customer has redeemed 3x at your store"                                                                        
                                                                                                                        
 ### 5. Retargeting Pool                                                                                                
                                                                                                                        
 - "We can retarget your redeemers with new offers"                                                                     
 - Build a local audience you can market to for future campaigns                                                        
                                                                                                                        
 ### 6. No Minimums, No Contracts                                                                                       
                                                                                                                        
 - "Try it for one week. If you get 0 redemptions, you pay $0."

 Missing Piece: Can You Guarantee Volume?                                                                               
                                                                                                                        
 The risk for merchants: "What if I get 0 redemptions?"                                                                 
                                                                                                                        
 Options:                                                                                                               
 - Minimum guarantee: "We'll drive at least 10 redemptions in your first week or your next campaign is free"            
 - Performance tier: Pay $2/redemption for first 50, $1.50 for 51-100 (volume discount)                                 
 - Success stories: Case studies from similar merchants
